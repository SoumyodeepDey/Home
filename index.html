Research Interest

Being a research scholar in a premier institute of India, IIT Madras, I was fortunate to have a well-established laboratory for my research work. While working with my guide Prem B. Bisht, my initial interest grew towards the nonlinear light matter interactions under ultrafast laser excitation. This includes the self-phase modulation and spectral broadening of ultrafast laser pulses. Under extreme condition, several higher order nonlinear effects such as stimulated Raman scattering (SRS), Four wave mixing (FWM) are also important phenomena. These phenomena motivates me to generate supercontinuum laser sources and its applications in finding nonlinear parameters as a function of frequencies by a well-known technique z-scan. For high repetition rate ultrafast pulses, alongside the optical effects, there exist significant thermal effects which leads to interesting phenomena while measuring nonlinear parameters of optical materials. I was able to investigate this thermal effects numerically by finite difference method and verified experimentally later. As the thermal effects due to continuous wave (CW) laser is a well-establish phenomena, I became motivated to investigate the pump-probe type geometry under such condition. As a result, a ring shaped beam has been generated similar to the Laguerre Gaussian vortex (LGV) beams. I have further investigated the phase profile of the thermally induced beam with the LGV beam and found distinctly different phase structure. This work was done in collaboration with Prof. S.P.Singh from the University of Arkansas, USA. Working with Prof. Singh, motivates me towards the structure beam generation and its application in quantum optics as well as optical trapping experiments. Also during my PhD tenure I was able to be a part of the project to build No-collinear Optical Parametric Amplifier (NOPA) and a Transient Absorption (TA) setup. During those work, the generation of superfluorescent ring from a BBO crystal motivates me to chose my carrier in quantum optics. Currently I am working on the following topics:

  • Optical Instrumentation for precision measurement
  • Structured Beam (Laguerre Gaussian Vortex type) generation and application
  • Supercontinuum Generation and it's applications
  • Femtosecond pulse broadening and autocorrelation
  • Thermo-optical study of optical materials due to intense light-matter interaction
  • Ultrafast dynamics and transient absorption spectroscopy
